## Top Recommendations

### Smith.ai AI Receptionist Starter ($95/month)

**Pros:**
- Human agent backup available (+$3/call)
- 10+ years experience, 17M+ calls handled
- Excellent CRM integrations (HubSpot, Salesforce)
- Lead qualification included
- 30-day money-back guarantee

**Cons:**
- Higher per-call pricing ($2.10-$2.40/call overage)
- Call-based pricing less predictable than minutes

**Best for:** Consultants who want the safety net of human backup for complex or high-stakes calls with C-level executives.

### Dialzara Business Pro ($99/month)

**Pros:**
- 220 minutes/month included
- Fast 10-15 minute setup
- 40+ natural voice options
- Strong Zapier/Make integrations
- Native Google Calendar integration

**Cons:**
- High overage rate ($0.48/min)
- No human backup option

**Best for:** Budget-conscious operators who want quick setup and don't need human escalation.

## Final Recommendation

**Start with Smith.ai AI Receptionist Starter at $95/month.** The human backup option is worth the marginal premium when your brand positioning is "trusted advisor to mid-market enterprises." One fumbled call with a CFO could cost you a $100K engagement.

If after 3 months you find you never need the human escalation, consider switching to Dialzara Business Pro ($99/month) for the additional minutes.
