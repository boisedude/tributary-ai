## Prioritized Recommendations

### National AI Consulting - TOP 3

**1. LinkedIn Organic Content**
- 80% of B2B leads come from LinkedIn
- Contrarian positioning perfect for thought leadership
- Investment: 5-10 hours/week
- Timeline: Build audience months 1-3, leads months 4-6
- Action: Post 3-4x weekly on AI simplification, coordination tax

**2. Cold Email + LinkedIn Outreach**
- Consulting firms: 7.88% reply rate (highest industry)
- Combined approach: 287% better results
- Investment: 10-15 hours/week
- Timeline: Responses month 1, pipeline months 2-4
- Action: 500 target companies, personalized sequences

**3. Referral Development**
- 84% of B2B buying starts with referral
- 11% conversion rate
- Investment: 2-3 hours/week
- Action: Activate Microsoft, Citrix, Confluent network

---

### Local MSP (Boise) - TOP 3

**1. Local SEO + Google Business Profile**
- 46% of Google searches seek local info
- Investment: 10 hours upfront, 3-5 hours/month
- Timeline: Rankings months 3-6
- Action: Optimize GBP, local citations, reviews

**2. Local Networking (BNI/Chamber)**
- One partner = 2-4 qualified leads monthly
- Investment: 4-6 hours/week
- Timeline: First referrals months 2-3
- Action: Join Chamber, evaluate BNI, partner with accountants/lawyers

**3. LinkedIn Local Outreach**
- Position as local IT expert
- Investment: 3-5 hours/week
- Timeline: Leads months 3-6
- Action: Connect with Boise business owners, local content

## Key Insights

1. **$25-35K consulting is easier to sell than MSP contracts** - High-ticket responds to thought leadership; MSP requires persistent local presence.

2. **Multi-channel wins** - Email + LinkedIn + phone delivers 287% better results than single-channel.

3. **Your contrarian positioning is an asset** - "AI should reduce tech spend" will generate engagement and polarize (good for attention).

4. **Time is scarcest resource** - Prioritize channels that compound (LinkedIn organic, SEO, referrals) over constant-feeding channels.
